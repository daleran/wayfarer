## UP NEXT

### AO: Weight System

Heavier modules decrease acceleration and turn rate. Top speed remains the same, but adding modules — especially heavy ones like cargo bays, armor inserts, and large reactors — increases the ship's mass and penalizes agility. Removing modules improves responsiveness. Feeds naturally into build tradeoffs: a lighter, stripped-down Hullbreaker vs. a fully kitted but sluggish one.
