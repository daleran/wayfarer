### Engine Module Brainstorming

Create these 5 engines based on the descriptions below. 


* **Makeshift Thermal Rocket**
    * **Description:** A makeshift, jury-rigged rocket engine burning fuel and oxygen cobbled together from a hodgepodge of scavenged parts. It works, but not well. 
    * **Stats:** Abysmal thrust, poor fuel efficiency, and very quality/reliability (breaks frequently).
    * **Value:** Very cheap to acquire. Typically a starter engine or something to get you going on the way.

* **Vintage Magplasma Thruster**
    * **Description:** An older yet highly advanced model engine dating back to the arrival/drift period that uses magnetic fields to turn fuel into a plasma and eject it at high speeds,
    * **Stats:** Excellent combination of thrust and fuel efficiency, but suffers from low reliability due to its age and hard to find parts. It has a thrust between the Ion Thruster and the To be named Rocket Engine. 

* **Standard Pattern Rocket Engine**
    * **Description:** A reliable workhorse for the masses. A mass manufactured design that has been around from before the exile. Manufactured by various small engine forges across Tyr. 
    * **Stats:** Average thrust, Average fuel efficency. Very reliable if built well.
    * **Value:** Cheaper than Milspec ones, better than a Makeshift. Good middle of the road. 

* **Milspec Rocket Engine**
    * **Description:** High-performance military-grade engine designed for fleet operations. Manufactured by the Prime Machinists Guild. A powerful, neutral body of engineers who control the precision ceramic kilns needed for high-output propulsion.
    * **Stats:** Very powerful with average reliability; however, it has very poor fuel efficiency as most military ships operate near fleets with onboard fuel facilities.

* **Cruising Ion Thruster**
    * **Description:** Designed for long-range cargo haulers and endurance transit.
    * **Stats:** Low thrust output, but exceptionally reliable and requiring very little fuel. Can run clear accross the system on a single tank at full speed.
